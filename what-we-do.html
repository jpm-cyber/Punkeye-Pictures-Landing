At a glance

Punkeye Pictures turns daily creative tasks—photos, sketches, short clips—into finished films. People use phones and simple tools. Staff get training and structure. No complex equipment. It takes as long as it takes.

  • Who it's for — Day programs, families, schools, community organizations serving people with developmental disabilities.
  • Week to week — One simple creative task per day, weekly check-ins, project builds at everyone's pace. We get people started, figure out what works for each person, run the project, then screen or share.
  • What people get — Routine, purpose, credit for their work, something to show. The person making the decisions holds the creative value, not whoever's holding the camera.
  • For organizations — Training for caregivers, repeatable structure, lower supply costs. A built-in activity that actually fills the schedule. We're still learning what works best, but the core model is there.
  • What you need — Smartphones and simple digital tools your staff already have. We provide training, support, oversight. No specialized instructors or expensive equipment.
