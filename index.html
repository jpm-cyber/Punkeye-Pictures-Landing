Redefining day programs for adults with developmental disabilities through creative, structured daily media projects.

Get Involved

Our Mission

We empower adults with developmental disabilities and their caregivers to tell their stories through daily micro‑tasks using smartphones. By combining structure, creativity and accessible technology, Punkeye Pictures fosters confidence, skills and authentic representation.

How It Works

Participants and caregivers capture short videos, photos or sounds using a unique QR code. Our automated system organises the files, freeing facilitators to focus on storytelling and connection. Over time, these micro‑contributions become compelling documentaries, music videos, animations and more.
