Punkeye Pictures

Project-Based Programming with Purpose

We're redefining day programs for adults with developmental disabilities through creative, structured daily video and media projects. Punkeye Pictures helps participants build skills, have fun, and tell their stories—one photo, one clip at a time.

Our pilot program is launching soon with select partner organizations. If you're a family member, caregiver, or organization looking to offer meaningful engagement, structure, and joy—you're in the right place.
