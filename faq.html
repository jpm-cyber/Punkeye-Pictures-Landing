FAQ

Questions we get a lot. Answers as we have them.

For organizations

What does it cost? How does licensing work?

We're still finalizing the model. Expect licensing per organization or per participant, plus training. We'll share specifics when we have a pilot partner and numbers that work. Get in touch if you want to be part of that conversation.

How long does a project typically run?

Weeks to months. Projects build at participant pace—one simple creative task per day, weekly check-ins. No fixed deadline. A typical film might take 6–12 weeks depending on the group.

What if a participant drops out?

Their contributions stay in the project. We don't delete work. If someone stops participating, we respect that—and whatever they've already contributed becomes part of the timeline. The rest of the group continues.

How much training do caregivers need?

We teach the model so it slots into existing routines. No specialized instructors. Staff use phones and simple tools they already have. Training is part of the implementation—we'll scope it with you.

Data & privacy

Where is participant media stored? Who has access?

Media stays local. We're not building a cloud platform that holds your data. Participant work is stored under your control; we help with the workflow. Nothing is shared beyond the project until participants and the cohort explicitly opt in.

What about consent for filming?

Ongoing consent, not a one-time release. Participation is driven by daily choice—whether to contribute or not. If someone chooses not to contribute, that's respected. We use an Own Voices approach: participants tell their own stories.

Rights & ownership

Who owns the final films?

Participants retain authorship. Creative value isn't who holds the camera—it's who makes the decisions. Media and stories stay under their control and the local cohort's. Nothing is shared beyond the project until they opt in.

For families

What's the family/individual offering?

We're building it. Same model: phones, simple tools, structure. Subscriptions or sessions with a loved one and their caregiver. We don't have pricing or format finalized yet. Express interest and we'll add you to the list.
