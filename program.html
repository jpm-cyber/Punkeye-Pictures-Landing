Our Program

Creative routines, everyday joy

Daily Micro‑Tasks

Participants complete small daily creative tasks—taking a photo, recording a sound, or filming a short video. Each task builds confidence and skills without overwhelming. Over time, these micro‑contributions are woven into personal documentaries, music videos and animations.

Caregiver‑Centric Design

Many clients cannot operate a smartphone on their own. Our QR system empowers caregivers: each participant has a personal code that caregivers can scan on their own devices to upload content. Multiple caregivers can contribute seamlessly, ensuring continuity in environments with rotating staff.

Automation & Support

Once uploaded, our scripts sort, rename and store files automatically, freeing facilitators to focus on engagement and storytelling instead of paperwork. Participants retain ownership of their work, and all data is securely backed up.
