Partners & Supporters

Together we make inclusion possible

Community Partners

We collaborate with social‑service agencies, day programs and community centres to deliver our program. Partner agencies gain engaging activities, participant empowerment and access to our platform and training. If you're a support organisation like CLBC, BACI or posAbilities, let's connect.

Funders & Sponsors

Punkeye Pictures is a social enterprise. We fund our work through facilitator licensing fees, grants and corporate sponsorships. Foundations like the Vancouver Foundation and companies like Vancity, FortisBC and Telus recognise the power of inclusive storytelling. We invite grant makers and sponsors to invest in our mission and help us scale.

Universities & Research

We welcome partnerships with universities and research centres. Film schools can provide interns and co‑produce projects; research institutes can measure impact and develop new assistive technologies. If you're from UBC, SFU, Emily Carr, Capilano University or another institution, we want to hear from you.
