Privacy

How we handle your information.

Information we collect

When you contact us by email or book a call, we receive your name, email address, and any message you send. We use this only to respond and to follow up about Punkeye Pictures.

Booking (Google Calendar & Meet)

Our booking page embeds Google Calendar appointment scheduling. When you book a call, Google collects the information you provide to schedule the event and add a Google Meet link. Google’s services are covered by Google’s privacy policy. We receive the booking details so we can conduct the call.

Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies or collect personal data for advertising. It helps us understand basic traffic (e.g. page views) without identifying individual visitors.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing. We may share information only as needed to provide services (e.g. with Google for scheduling and video) or when required by law.
